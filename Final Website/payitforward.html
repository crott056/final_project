Pay It
Forward

To “Pay it Foward” can be
defined as someone doing
something kind for you, yet
instead of just doing
something for them in return,
you do another deed for
someone else as well. As an
alternative to just paying the
person back you can spread
the joy on to another person.

“It has been proven that acts
of kindness build exponentially
in a community.” When people
believe that one good deed
deserves another, a chain
reaction is made, influencing
everyone around us in a positive
light. It is a small step one
can take to help make the
world a bit of a better place.

Tips

Giving someone a
compliment.

Making a donation
to a charity.

Helping a stranger.

Sharing a memory.

Giving an
unexpected gift.

Sponsoring
a child.

Actively listening
to someone.

Volunteering
your time.
